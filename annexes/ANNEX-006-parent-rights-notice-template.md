# ANNEX-006: Parent Rights Notice Template

## Purpose

This template provides sample notice language that schools can use to inform parents about their rights regarding student data when **[Company Name]**'s services are used. This notice supports FERPA, COPPA, and state law notice requirements.

## Usage Notes

- Schools customize this template for their specific context
- Schools determine the distribution method (website, handbook, letter)

# PARENT NOTICE: STUDENT DATA PRIVACY

## What This Notice Is About

Your child's school uses educational technology products provided by **[Company Name]** to support learning. This notice explains how your child's information is collected, used, and protected, and describes your rights as a parent.

---

## Information We Collect

When your child uses **[Company Name]**'s services, the following types of information may be collected:

**Student Information:**
- Name and student ID
- Grade level and school
- Class and teacher assignments
- Email address (if applicable)

**Educational Information:**
- Learning activities and progress
- Assessment scores and results
- Content created by your child

**Usage Information:**
- Login times and session duration
- Features used
- Technical information (device type, browser)

---

## How Information Is Used

Your child's information is used to:

- ✓ Provide educational services to your child
- ✓ Generate reports for teachers and school administrators
- ✓ Improve the educational products and services
- ✓ Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

---

## How Information Is Protected

**[Company Name]** protects your child's information by:

- **Limiting access** – Only authorized school staff and **[Company Name]** employees with a need to know can access student data
- **Encrypting data** – Information is encrypted when transmitted and stored
- **Training employees** – Staff are trained on privacy and security practices
- **Following the law** – **[Company Name]** complies with FERPA, COPPA, and state student privacy laws

---

## What We Don't Do

**[Company Name]** does NOT:

- ✗ **Sell** your child's information to anyone
- ✗ Use your child's information for **advertising** to students
- ✗ Create **profiles** about your child for commercial purposes
- ✗ **Share** your child's information except as needed to provide educational services

---

## Your Rights as a Parent

Under federal and state law, you have the right to:

### Review Your Child's Information
You may request to review the educational records and personal information the school and **[Company Name]** maintain about your child.

**How to request:** Contact your school's administration office.

### Request Correction
If you believe any information about your child is inaccurate or misleading, you may request that it be corrected.

**How to request:** Submit a written request to your school explaining the information you believe is inaccurate.

### Request Deletion
You may request that your child's personal information be deleted.

**How to request:** Contact your school's administration office. Note that deletion may affect your child's ability to use the educational service.

### Refuse Further Collection
You may refuse to allow further collection of your child's information.

**How to request:** Contact your school's administration office. Note that this may affect your child's ability to use the educational service.

---

## Schools Act on Your Behalf

Under COPPA (the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), schools may consent to the collection of children's personal information on behalf of parents when:

- The service is used for educational purposes
- Information is not used for commercial purposes unrelated to education

Your school has reviewed **[Company Name]**'s privacy practices and determined they meet legal requirements for protecting student data.

---

## How Long Is Information Kept?

**[Company Name]** keeps your child's information only as long as:
- Your child's school has a contract with **[Company Name]**, OR
- The school directs **[Company Name]** to keep it

When the school's contract ends or the school requests deletion, **[Company Name]** deletes your child's information.

## For New York Parents

*[Include if school is in New York]*

Under New York Education Law 2-d, parents have a Parents' Bill of Rights for Data Privacy and Security. Your school has posted this Bill of Rights on its website. Key rights include:

- Your child's personally identifiable information cannot be sold or released for commercial purposes
- You have the right to inspect and review your child's education records
- State and federal laws protect your child's personally identifiable information
- You may file complaints with the school or the Chief Privacy Officer at the NY State Education Department
