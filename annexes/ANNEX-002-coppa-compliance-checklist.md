# ANNEX-002: COPPA Compliance Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist provides a comprehensive verification of **[Company Name]**'s compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 16 CFR Part 312.

## Scope

This checklist applies to all products and services used by children under 13 or directed to children.
